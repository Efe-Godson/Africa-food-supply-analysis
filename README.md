<h1>📊 Africa Food Supply Analysis</h1>

<h2>📌 Overview</h2>
<p>
This project explores food supply, production, and nutritional trends across African countries 
using historical data from the Food Balance Sheets dataset.
</p>

<p>
The analysis focuses on understanding how food availability, protein supply, and production patterns 
vary across regions and over time.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>🎯 Objectives</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Analyze food supply trends across African countries</li>
  <li>Examine protein supply distribution by region</li>
  <li>Identify patterns in production, imports, and consumption</li>
  <li>Explore year-over-year changes in key food metrics</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📂 Dataset</h2>
<p><strong>Source:</strong> Food Balance Sheets (Africa)</p>
<ul>
  <li>Country (Area)</li>
  <li>Food items</li>
  <li>Supply metrics (production, imports, exports)</li>
  <li>Nutritional indicators (protein, fat, calories)</li>
  <li>Yearly data (2014–2018)</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🛠️ Tools & Technologies</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Python</li>
  <li>Pandas</li>
  <li>NumPy</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>🔍 Key Analysis</h2>

<h3>1. Data Cleaning & Preparation</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Handled missing values across multiple years</li>
  <li>Standardized dataset structure</li>
  <li>Filtered relevant metrics such as protein supply</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Aggregation & Grouping</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Grouped data by country and element</li>
  <li>Calculated total food supply across years</li>
  <li>Performed country-level protein supply analysis</li>
</ul>

<h3>3. Nutritional Insights</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Analyzed protein supply per capita</li>
  <li>Explored fat supply trends</li>
  <li>Examined caloric availability across regions</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Country-Level Analysis</h3>
<ul>
  <li>Compared food supply across African countries</li>
  <li>Identified high vs low supply regions</li>
  <li>Highlighted variations in food availability</li>
</ul>

<hr>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Food supply increased across most countries over time</li>
  <li>Significant disparities exist in nutritional availability</li>
  <li>Protein supply varies widely between regions</li>
  <li>Some countries show declining trends in key metrics</li>
</ul>
